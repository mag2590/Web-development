Fun Facts about developing a website

  • Your website will only last you about two years.
  • Your website looks different on other people’s computers.
  • Your website’s source code influences your search engine ranking.
  • Images can have a positive or negative impact on your site.
  • There’s a big difference between responsive design and mobile design.
  • Your website’s design influences visitor behavior.
